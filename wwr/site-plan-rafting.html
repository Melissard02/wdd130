Overview

Purpose

Here at White Mountain Rafting we are dedicated to providing family fun, adventure all in a safe enviornment. Our expert guides are set up to provide fun experiences from amature rafter to professionals.

Audience

Are you looking to find adventure and fun in your life? White Mountain Rafting is the way to do it, completely safe while thrilling! Do you love the outdoors and being apart of nature? Our tours will take you along the sites of river where you can see beautiful plants and majestic animals! Are struggling to find an activity that the whole family can participate in? All adults and children over the age of 12 are welcome to join our tours!
