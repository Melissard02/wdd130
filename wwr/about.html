History

White Mountain Rafting was started as a small family opporation located along the Colorado River. As the family grew so did their love of adventure. They expanded their business and hire more trained guides and more excursion along different parts of the river. Today the business thrives with over thirty guides and five seperate tours. We hope you will join us on the journey!
